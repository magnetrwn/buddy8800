Detailed Description

template<usize start_adr, usize base_clock = 19200>
class serial_card< start_adr, base_clock >

Based on the MC6850 ACIA.

Warning
Out of range addresses are not checked, they should be checked by the bus instead, to avoid calling in_range() twice.
